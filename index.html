ComiDv

Read Aloud & Translate Comics and Novels

Powerful App for Reading Comics and Novels

ComiDv is an app that helps you read aloud the text in comics and novels through images or files. Whether you are reading English, Japanese, Korean, Chinese or other languages, the app can support translation of over 50 languages ​​and Text-to-Speech.

Main Functions

  • Read text in images aloud (OCR + Text-to-Speech)
  • Translate text in images to your chosen language
  • Open text files, images or comic folders
  • Enter text manually to read
  • Minimalist interface, optimized for users
